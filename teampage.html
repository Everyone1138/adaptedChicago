Meet Our Team

Clay Kerr

Founder & Creative Director

Clay's journey in film production took shape amidst the glitz and glamor of Hollywood, where he had the privilege of working on the sets of hit shows like "Modern Family" and "It’s Always Sunny." What set Clay apart in the hustle and bustle of the industry was his exceptional adaptability. Then, the world was hit by a pandemic, and uncertainty loomed large. It was during this tumultuous time that the concept of "Adaptability" struck a profound chord with Clay. Driven by a deep appreciation for individuality, creativity, and the unique talents within each of us, Clay made a significant decision. He bid farewell to Hollywood and returned to his roots in Chicago to establish Adapted. At Adapted, Clay’s mission is clear: to partner with brands and businesses, working closely with them to create video content that does more than just showcase their gifts and stories. He crafts content that resonates with the world while adapting to the distinctive personality traits, goals, and visions of his clients.
